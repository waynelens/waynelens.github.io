# Midnight Platform

The station is almost silent, but the light still moves. Every reflection across tile and steel becomes its own layer of motion.

I kept the framing centered and restrained, letting symmetry carry most of the weight while the shadows fill in the narrative.

The sequence works best when each frame feels deliberate, almost like a pause between two trains that never appear.
