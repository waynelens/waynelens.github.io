# Station Glass

Glass separates every subject into two stories: what is in front of the camera and what appears as a faint duplicate behind it.

The strongest frames came from waiting for a brief overlap between people, typography, and moving light.

I kept contrast moderate so the layered reflections stay readable instead of collapsing into a single dark mass.
