My uni junior, who’s usually all about hiking, wanted to try snorkeling for the first time.

Bitoujiao is honestly the perfect spot—calm waters, colorful coral underneath, and the way the light changes throughout the day makes it feel almost magical.

With a float buoy, even beginners can enjoy it safely. Hope my friends get to fall in love with the ocean like this. 🌊
