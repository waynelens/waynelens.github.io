# After Rain Arcade

Rain turns a familiar block into a light source. Pavement carries color farther than any storefront sign can on its own.

The wider format helped preserve the movement of people crossing through frame edges without losing context.

This series depends on restraint: fewer subjects, longer observation, and just enough blur to suggest motion.
